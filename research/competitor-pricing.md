## Detailed Competitor Analysis

### 1. Nansen

**Overview:** Nansen is the leading on-chain analytics platform with comprehensive multi-chain support including Solana. It pioneered the "Smart Money" concept and offers sophisticated wallet labeling and tracking.

**Key Differentiators:**
- 400M+ labeled addresses across chains
- AI-powered signals and pattern recognition
- Smart Money tracking (top 5,000 performing wallets)
- Cross-platform (web + mobile)

**Wallet Scoring Approach:**
- Realized profit tracking
- Win rate analysis across market cycles
- Performance persistence metrics
- Entity categorization (whales, smart money, exchanges)

**Pricing Evolution:**
- Recently reduced pricing significantly (from $999/month to $49/month)
- Made premium features accessible to retail users
- API credits sold separately at $0.001/credit

## Market Gaps & Opportunities

### Identified Gaps:

1. **Comprehensive Wallet Scoring:** No single platform offers a holistic wallet trust/trustworthiness score combining behavior, profitability, and risk metrics specifically for Solana.

2. **Agent-to-Agent Trust Layer:** No existing solution provides a scoring mechanism designed specifically for AI agents to evaluate wallet counterparts.

3. **Transparent Scoring Methodology:** Most platforms keep their scoring algorithms opaque. There's opportunity for open, auditable scoring mechanisms.

4. **Integration with Agent Frameworks:** Limited MCP (Model Context Protocol) support for agent integration beyond Nansen's recent offering.

### Pricing Insights:

- **Entry Point:** Most tools offer free tiers, but meaningful access starts at $49-69/month
- **Enterprise Range:** $200-999/month for professional API access
- **API Pricing:** Credit-based systems are standard ($0.001 per call typical)
- **Solana-Native Tools:** Tend to be cheaper or free compared to multi-chain platforms

### API Accessibility:

- **Free APIs:** RugCheck (completely free), Dune (limited), Nansen (100 credits)
- **Premium APIs:** Most require paid subscriptions starting at $49/month
- **Enterprise APIs:** Custom pricing for high-volume access
